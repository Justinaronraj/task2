About us

Since its production in 2006, the F-35 has embodied the most advanced fighter aircraft development. The Lockheed Martin F-35 Lightning II fighter jet boasts stealth features, the most powerful aircraft engine, and a highly advanced radar. The pilots even wear special augmented reality helmets, giving them an unprecedented level of situational awareness. The F-35 adopts stealth and sensor fusion capabilities from all aspects to the maximum. This makes him nearly invincible in beyond-visual-range combat. The F-35 scored a kill ratio of 20:1 in one exercise, meaning the aircraft won 20 of its 21 engagements.This achievement is the result of the F-35's situational awareness function which can track and attack targets in all directions. Trailing the F-35 doesn't necessarily mean it's safe from missiles. The F-35 fighter jet has also been successful in exporting to a number of countries.
